Intro

With over three years of professional experience in machine learning, where I have led and co-led research projects resulting in publications at top-tier conferences and one patent, I’m extremely excited to be applying for the Research Scientist position at InstaDeep. After completing a PhD in high dimensional statistics with a focus on theoretical understanding of graph-based and kernel unsupervised learning methods, I moved to an industrial lab at Huawei, London to pursue my desire to perform fundamental research in Machine learning and apply it to real-world problems. After more than three years working on different research projects at Huawei, I am searching for a role that will offer me the opportunity to work on impactful research, and the chance to collaborate with other like-mind ambitious researchers. As such I'm thrilled to have the opportunity to offer my talents to InstaDeep. I’m particularly excited doing AI research on computational biology topics such as drug design, protein folding as this has direct impact on the population health and will be without doubt one of the key component of the future of medicine and biology. Although I have no prior professional experience in computational biology, I believe that my skills in AI/Machine learning would be a driver to quickly tackle research in this challenging area. While working at Huawei, I have been involved across teams work on various projects around: unsupervised learning, multi-task learning, generative models and deep learning, mainly focused on computer vision applications. Alongside this I have worked on proving theoretical guarantees of some of the algorithms used.awesome work.
